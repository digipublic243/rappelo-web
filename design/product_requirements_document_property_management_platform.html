# Product Requirements Document: Premium B2B Property Management Platform ## 1. Executive Summary A modern, API-first, role-aware property management web app designed for Landlords and Tenants. The platform prioritizes high-trust, simple-to-use workflows over generic CRUD, focusing on status-driven actions and professional aesthetics. ## 2. User Roles - **Landlord:** Manages portfolios, properties, units, tenants, and financial performance. - **Tenant:** Manages their own bookings, leases, and payments via phone + OTP login. ## 3. Core Domain Model - **Property:** A physical asset owned by a landlord. - **Unit:** A specific space within a property (room, apartment, etc.). - **Booking:** A request/reservation for a unit. - **Lease:** A formal agreement connecting a unit to a tenant (distinct from a booking). - **Payment:** Financial transactions tied to leases/bookings. - **Pricing Rules:** Highly configurable (day/week/month/custom) with deposits and advance payment requirements. ## 4. Visual & UX Direction - **Style:** Premium, simple, B2B back-office feel. - **Palette:** Soft neutrals, high-quality typography, accessible contrast. - **Layout:** Modern card/table hybrid, rounded but restrained components. - **Principles:** Clarity over density, status-driven actions, guided flows. ## 5. Screen Map ### Landlord Experience 1. **Sign In / Sign Up:** Professional entry point. 2. **Dashboard:** Portfolio overview, revenue, occupancy, and "Needs Attention" widgets. 3. **Property List:** Card/Table hybrid showing occupancy and health. 4. **Property Detail:** Deep dive into units, history, and property-level stats. 5. **Unit Detail/Config:** Pricing rules (cadence, deposit, advance), status management. 6. **Tenant List:** Database of all current and past tenants. 7. **Tenant Detail:** Lease history, payment reliability, and contact info. 8. **Booking Requests:** Inbox-style list for approval/rejection. 9. **Booking Review (Drawer):** Contextual review of applicant and terms. 10. **Lease List:** Overview of all active and pending agreements. 11. **Lease Detail:** Timeline, documents, and specific financial terms. 12. **Payments List:** Ledger of all transactions across the portfolio. 13. **Payment Link Modal:** Quick action to generate secure payment URLs. 14. **Reminder Modal:** Status-driven communication for overdue amounts. 15. **Reports/Stats:** Lightweight charts for revenue and occupancy trends. ### Tenant Experience 16. **Tenant Login:** Clean phone + OTP interface. 17. **Tenant Dashboard:** Upcoming payments, active lease status, quick support. 18. **My Bookings:** History and status of requested stays. 19. **My Leases:** Active and historical legal agreements. 20. **My Payments:** Billing history and "Pay Now" actions. 21. **Property Booking Flow:** Multi-step guided flow to select unit and request stay. ## 6. Key UX Decisions - **Status Badges:** Color-coded (e.g., Green: Paid/Occupied, Yellow: Pending/Reserved, Red: Overdue/Vacant). - **Contextual Actions:** Buttons change based on the 'Status' (e.g., a "Pending" booking shows "Approve/Reject", while an "Approved" one shows "Generate Lease"). - **Empty States:** Instructive guidance for new users (e.g., "Add your first property to get started").
